Return & Refund Policy

1. Return Eligibility

To be eligible for a return or refund, the following conditions must be met:
- Items must be unused, unwashed, and in their original packaging (with tags intact).
- Returns must be requested within 7 calendar days of receiving your order.
- Custom-made or personalized items are non-returnable (unless defective).
- Defective items (e.g., damaged, wrong size/style) are eligible for return/exchange.

2. How to Request a Return

Step 1: Log in to your account and go to "My Orders" to find the order you want to return.
Step 2: Click "Request Return" and fill in the return reason (e.g., wrong size, defective).
Step 3: We will review your request within 1-2 business days and send a return confirmation.
Step 4: Pack the item securely (with original packaging) and ship it back to our warehouse (address provided in return confirmation).
Step 5: Once we receive and inspect the item, we will process your refund or exchange.

3. Refund Information

- Refunds will be processed to your original payment method (credit card, digital wallet, etc.).
- Refund processing time: 3-5 business days after we receive and inspect your returned item.
- Shipping fees for returns are borne by the customer (unless the return is due to our error, e.g., wrong item).
- Partial refunds are available for items with minor defects (at our discretion).

4. Exchange Policy

If you want to exchange an item (e.g., wrong size, wrong color), follow the same return process and note "Exchange" in your request.
Exchanges are subject to stock availability. If the desired item is out of stock, we will offer a full refund instead.

5. Non-Returnable Items

The following items are non-returnable and non-refundable:
- Customized/personalized items (e.g., engraved products, made-to-order items).
- Perishable goods (if applicable).
- Items without original packaging or tags.
- Items that have been used, washed, or damaged by the customer.
