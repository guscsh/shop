Payment Policy

1. Accepted Payment Methods

We support the following payment options: Credit Card, Debit Card, Online Bank Transfer and Digital Wallet. All transactions are secured and encrypted to protect your payment information.

2. Payment Process

After you complete your order, you will be redirected to the secure payment page. Please finish payment within 24 hours, otherwise your order will be automatically canceled.

3. Payment Security

We do not store your full card details on our server. All payment data is processed by certified payment providers. Your financial information is fully protected.

4. Refund & Payment Rules

Refunds will be returned to your original payment account within 3-7 working days after approval. Partial payment or installment is not supported for current orders.

5. Payment Failure

If your payment fails, please check your account balance, network status or card limit. You can retry payment in your order history page.
